Interop is an early stage venture fund backing teams building applications and protocols in the Interchain economy, know canonically as the Cosmos Network. We invest at pre-seed and seed stage with ticket sizes between $250 and $500k.

Web3 technologies are creating a new application paradigm where users control their data, their identity and their assets. The Interchain is a network of interconnected blockchains where assets, data and state flow freely. The Cosmos Network and its technology stack rest at the center of this vision and are attracting seasoned entrepreneurs and rockstar developers who believe in a multi-chain future.

We look for teams delivering amazing user and developer experiences, while contributing positively to the ecosystem's growth and attractiveness.
